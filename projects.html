Projects

Enterprise Data Governance and Quality Management

I spearheaded and established a comprehensive framework to enhance data trustworthiness, accessibility, and compliance. This critical initiative involved the successful implementation of Microsoft Purview as the core enterprise data catalogue platform. I leveraged specific Microsoft Purview capabilities, including the Unified Data Map for automated data discovery and scanning, the Data Catalog for rich metadata management and business glossary creation, and Automated Classification for identifying sensitive data. Post-implementation, I collaborated closely with various business units and data domain experts across the agency to drive platform adoption, embed data stewardship best practices, and ensure Microsoft Purview served as the central hub for improving data understanding, quality, and regulatory adherence across the organization's diverse data landscape.

Business Intelligence Implementation

Led a multidisciplinary team in the end-to-end development and implementation of a custom Finance analytics solution for a Tier 1 Bank, successfully achieving a 60% reduction in reporting tool implementation and subscription fees and increasing solution delivery rate by 20%. This involved leading requirements gathering, designing data governance principles, coordinating report development in Power BI, collaborating on UI/UX design with Figma, contributing to data aggregation scripting, supporting backend-frontend integration (JavaScript, jQuery), and facilitating user adoption sessions.

Data Migration Assurance

Led a team on the Data Migration Assurance project for a telecommunication network in Rwanda, focused on ensuring the complete and accurate migration of critical business data from a legacy to a target system using ETL scripts and a vendor's data migration tool. This involved leading the development of data reconciliation and migration tracking tools, designing data migration requirements, optimizing ETL scripts for timely data cutover, translating business needs into specifications for data store/warehouse/mart design, and collaborating effectively with cross-functional teams and international vendors to ensure successful data transformation and migration outcomes.

Enterprise Data Strategy Design and Implementation

At a major financial institution, I spearheaded the end-to-end process of defining a robust, scalable data strategy and overseeing its technical execution. This involved architecting and implementing a modern data platform leveraging key Azure technologies, including Azure Data Lake Storage Gen2 for scalable data ingestion and storage, Azure Databricks for large-scale data processing and transformation, Azure Synapse Analytics as the cloud data warehouse for integrated analytics, and Azure Purview for comprehensive data governance and cataloging. Post-implementation, I collaborated closely with various business units, acting as a bridge between technical capabilities and business needs, to ensure the platform effectively supported their analytical requirements, enabled data-driven decision-making, and fostered a culture of data utilisation across the organisation.

Advanced Data Science Secondment

Developed and deployed models for customer segmentation, churn, CLV, product recommendations, and revenue drivers analysis, delivering actionable insights that enhanced customer management and contributed to business growth.
